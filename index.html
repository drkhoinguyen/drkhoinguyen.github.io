Biography

Khoi Nguyen is a PhD in Computer Science graduating from Oregon State University. His research interests include computer vision and machine learning. His current research focuses on applying deep learning approaches in computer vision including 3D Reconstruction, Segmentation, Few-shot Learning, and Unsupervised/Self-supervised Learning.
